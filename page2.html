Wheel in the United States

In the US, wheel gymnastics is very obscure. The only notable wheel gymnastics gyms are in Chicago, IL and Madison, WI. The US national team has only ever earned 2 medals, both in vault. The most prominent gym wheel club is located in Chicago, IL and has recently performed at events such as the Thanksgiving Day Parade, the Von Steuben Parade, and the 2024 US Gymnastics Olympic trials.
